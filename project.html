NEON ABSTRACT

3D • Art Direction • Concept

Brief

Reimagine the future of digital art through the lens of cyberpunk aesthetics, focusing on light, texture, and futuristic forms.

We embarked on a journey to explore the boundaries between the physical and digital worlds. Using procedural generation and advanced rendering techniques, we created a series of abstract forms that feel both organic and synthetic.

The use of neon lighting against dark, textured backgrounds creates a sense of depth and mystery, inviting the viewer to contemplate the nature of reality in a digital age.
